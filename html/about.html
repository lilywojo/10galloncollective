About

So who are we?

10 Gallon Collective is band created from a collective love of good music,
art, and making lasting memories. Just a group of
best friends based in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania,
we were born from Saturday night improv sessions and spontaneous jams.
But what we like to do most is play our favorite songs live
in front of an awesome audience. Come see us at local Bethlehem
bars, restuarants, and breweries to check us out!

Meet the members.

David Karp

Dave is the lead guitarist. He started playing pretty recently and
and enjoys improving his mad skills. Likes: any song with
a good solo, seventh cords, obscure card games, cooking.

Maria Saldutti

Maria is the drummer. She is self taught
and jams with soul and passion. Likes: drum fills,
fast tempo songs, turkey bacon, cartoons.

Shane McGorty

Shane is the bassist. He has been playing the longest
and is great at improvising no matter what the song is.
Likes: spicy riffs, ignoring pre-written
basslines for songs, backpacks, hot dogs.

Cakes

We give Cakes an unplugged keyboard and he does vocals
sometimes. Can improv like nobody's business.
Could be a movie star if he could get out of this place.
Likes: Jaws, Harry Potter, surfing.

Pat Mclaughlin

Pat is the rhythm guitarist. He's been playing
acoustic guitar for a few years and is pretty darn impressive.
Likes: classic rock, barre chords, rugby, mullets.
